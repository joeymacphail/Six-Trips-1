Bangladesh: The land of the Lilies

Hi, I'm Sheepa, and I'm from Bangladesh. You can find more information about Bangladesh below!

Hi, I'm Sheepa, and I come from Bangladesh. Bangladesh is referred to as the land of rivers and is home to over 700 rivers. Approximately 50% of the population are farmers, and tea farming, in particular, is important to us. Bangladesh is one of the world's largest tea exporters. I feel quite connected to my country as I have visited frequently growing up. Village life is much slower and is a nice break from the business of city life. Many people associate the Royal Bengal Tiger with Bangladesh as it is our national animal. Our national flower is the Water Lily, also known as "Shapla" in Bengali. The flower is associated with purity and serenity.

Things Bangladesh is famous for:

  1. The vibrant history
  2. Beautiful agricultural landscape
  3. Some of the oldest Islamic, Buddhist, and Hindu archaeological relics
  4. Dhaka, the most densely populated city on Earth
  5. Being in a country with the longest female-led government
  6. The Kacchi Biriyani!
  7. The Korma
  8. And most of all, the kind-hearted and lovely people
